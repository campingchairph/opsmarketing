Your role in campaigns

You execute, not decide. Every campaign action requires prior written approval from the Marketing Lead. Flawless execution and proactive status communication is your job.

Email campaign — 6-phase execution flow
1
Receive approved brief + content
Never start without written approval. Confirm: subject line, sender name, send time, list segment.
2
Build list in CRM / email platform
Upload contacts, apply segmentation. Check compliance — consent, opt-outs, financial services rules.
3
Set up email using approved template
Apply correct logo, disclaimer, formatting. No off-template design decisions.
4
Send test + QC checklist always
Broken links? Correct sender? Disclaimer visible? Rendering on mobile?
5
Get final sign-off before scheduling
Document approval (email or shared doc). Only then schedule.
6
Post-send: pull performance report
Open rate, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes. Add to tracker. Brief Marketing Lead on results.
